1.5 Problem 4 - Standard Form

\begin{align*} \min 3x_1 + 2x_2 + x_3 + 4x_4\\ \mbox{s.t. } x_1 + x_2- x_3 + x_4 &\geq 5\\ 2x_1 - 4x_2 + 3x_3 + x_4&= 10\\ -x_1 + 2x_2+ \phantom{x_3} +x_4 &\leq 8\\ x_2 + x_4 \geq -3\\ x_1, x_3\geq 0\\ x_2, x_4 & \mbox{ Unrestricted in Sign (Free)}\\ \end{align*}

(a) Convert the problem to standard form.

  • x_2 = e-f, e,f >= 0
  • x_4 = g-h, g,h >= 0
  • turn min into max

--->

-max -3x_1 - 2e + 2f - x_3 - 4g + 4h

s.t.

  • x_1 + e - f - x_3 + g - h >= 5
  • 2x_1 - 4e + 4f + 3x_3 + g - h = 10
  • -x_1 + 2e + 2f + g - h <= 8
  • e - f + g - h >= -3
  • x_1, x_3, e, f, g, h >=0
  • replace equality with two inequalitie
  • flip >=

--->

standard form

$\begin{align} \underset{x_1,e,f,x_3,g,h}{-\max}\ & -3x_1 - 2e + 2f - x_3 - 4g + 4h & \\ \text{s.t.} \ & -x_1 - e + f + x_3 - g + h \leq -5 &\\ & 2x_1 - 4e + 4f + 3x_3 + g - h \leq 10 &\\ & -2x_1 + 4e - 4f - 3x_3 - g + h \leq -10 &\\ & -x_1 + 2e + 2f + g - h \leq 8 &\\ & -e + f - g + h \leq 3 & \\ & x_1, x_3, e, f, g, h \geq 0& \end{align}$
